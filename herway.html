I'm a Computer Science student at Strathmore University with a passion for building technology that solves real-world problems — across full-stack engineering, cloud, artificial intelligence, IoT, and software architecture.

I enjoy taking ideas from concept to deployment, combining thoughtful system design with clean, maintainable code.
